Hello, Mode Mobile 👋

I'm applying for the General Manager, Mobile Games role.

I've built, scaled and run a portfolio of casual and puzzle games - and I want to do it again with Mode Mobile.

Portfolio-level games operations

At Denki, I led the design and production of 100+ shipped casual and puzzle games across every platform that mattered - iOS, Android, Facebook, console, interactive TV and HTML5. This wasn't one game at a time. It was a portfolio operation: managing multiple simultaneous dev teams and live titles; deciding what to build and how; balancing game quality, development velocity and cost efficiency; and building a lean production model that could ship polished games at volume with tiny teams.

I produced Quarrel from vertical slice to BAFTA-winning IP, scaling the team and establishing the studio's original IP development process. I also shipped licensed titles for DreamWorks, LEGO, Pixar, Mattel, Nickelodeon, Cartoon Network and more - each with its own commercial constraints, partner expectations and delivery timelines.

Growth, monetisation & economics

I understand how casual game economics work at the design level - retention loops, engagement mechanics, session architecture, monetisation balance, lifetime value. At Battlepass Labs, I designed a rewards and engagement engine as a web layer on top of existing live games, working directly with the economics of player attention, brand partner ROI and platform monetisation. I understand how rewarded distribution fundamentally changes the economics - when the user acquisition channel is the product itself, every design decision is a growth decision.

As a strategic advisor for Crowd Legends (532 Games), I shaped the GTM monetisation, feature prioritisation and commercial positioning for an F2P mobile title. I think in systems - ARPU, retention curves and acquisition economics are product design inputs for me, not just metrics to report.

Partnerships & deals

At InGAME, I built valuable partnerships with game studios, technology companies, universities and government funders - 177 R&D projects, £85M in economic impact, 16x ROI. I've structured collaboration agreements, managed multi-stakeholder relationships and evaluated strategic opportunities across the games ecosystem for twenty years. I know how developer, publisher and platform partnerships actually work because I've sat on every side of the table.

AI for studio productivity

I've rebuilt my entire production methodology around agentic AI workflows - using Claude, custom automation pipelines and purpose-built skills as core production infrastructure rather than bolt-on tools. This isn't about generating concept art. It's about compressing the entire production cycle: research, specification, prototyping, iteration, marketing and supporting documentation. A lean team using AI properly can outperform a team five times its size. I'm developing this framework now and it's how I'd approach building Mode's gaming operation.

What I'd bring

I don't need structure, playbooks or guardrails. I've built organisations from zero - InGAME from a blank sheet of paper to 16x ROI impact, Battlepass Labs from idea to validated proof of concept, Denki's casual game production model from first principles. I make decisions quickly, own the outcomes and build the teams and systems to scale what works. Your model - games as an engagement surface inside a rewards ecosystem - is a product architecture problem I find genuinely exciting and I want to be the person who makes it work.
