Sean Taylor

Making games and interactive amusements for 30 years.
I also advise, write, build labs and run experiments.

  • Early career at DMA Design (now Rockstar North) during the creation of Grand Theft Auto - one of the most commercially successful entertainment franchises in history. Credits include GTA, GTA 2 and Body Harvest.
  • Produced Quarrel from vertical slice to award-winning IP at boutique studio Denki - scaling the team, establishing the studio's original IP development process and winning a BAFTA Scotland, BAFTA Games nomination and three TIGA Awards including Best Game.
  • Built the UK's national games innovation centre from the ground up - 177 R&D projects, £85M in economic impact, 16x return on public investment in five years (2018-2023).
  • Published author: Punk Playthings: Provocations for 21st Century Game Makers (CRC Press, 2017)
  • 30 years industry experience, shipping 100+ games across console, mobile, web and emerging platforms - equally effective at board-level stakeholder engagement, leading small agile teams, and hands-on prototyping.
