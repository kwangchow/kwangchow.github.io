Experience

The Producers was founded by Sean Taylor to solve a problem for clients that kept cropping up in his previous life as Executive Producer at BAFTA-winning game studio, Denki - whilst play becomes more pervasive a lot of organisations struggle when they turn their hand to making games, or even their products more playful.

Making a game isn't complicated, it's just hard. To succeed you need a compass, a sherpa, an impresario. Your multiplier for execution.

To me, ideas are worth nothing unless executed. They are just a multiplier. Execution is worth millions.
