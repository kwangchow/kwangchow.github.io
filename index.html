Sean Taylor - Award-Winning Interactive Producer

I'm a digital producer, product leader and strategist.

I have spent twenty years planning, designing and producing interactive entertainment experiences, products, and services across the web, mobile, television, console and social.

Currently, I am building the PlayHaus, a pop-up game entrepreneurship incubator, and writing my first book, Punk Game Making for CRC Press (Q4 2017).

Previously, I helped a lot of good people turn ideas into interactive experiences, including:

  • helping Denki make the BAFTA-winning word game, Quarrel.
  • helping the Sophia George, V&A Designer in Residence, make Strawberry Thief, an experimental plaything based on the work of William Morris.
  • helping Mind Candy make the hit music video kids app, Moshi Music.
  • helping Turbulenz deliver gorgeous, console-quality, HTML5 browser games.
  • helping Reagent Games prototype their forthcoming, disruptive, online game.
  • helping edtech startup, Systematic Learning produce a playable investor showcase of their pioneering EYFS phonics programme, Tap Tap Bat.
  • helping DMA Design create the original Grand Theft Auto.

Let’s collaborate! I’m not doing too much consultancy these days but if you need a strategic thinker, project leader or mentor to help realise your vision of a more awesome, playful future, then let’s have a chat.
